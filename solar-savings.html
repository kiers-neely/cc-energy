Methodology aligned with Mechatron Energy Toolbase analysis (4/3/2026). Cost at $3.77/W DC · ITC 50% (30% base + 10% domestic content + 10% energy community) · MACRS 5-year half-year convention on reduced depreciable basis (gross − 50% × ITC) · federal tax rate adjustable (default 30%) · O&M $0.02/W-DC escalating 4%/yr with inverter replacement assumed yr 16 · panel degradation 0.5%/yr · utility escalation default 7%/yr · discount rate default 5% · 20-year horizon. Bill savings use 22.84¢/kWh effective rate (blended all-in rate minus non-bypassable PCIA + Wildfire = 1.08¢/kWh) on usable kWh derived from CCE tracker simulation vs Green Button 15-min interval data. ITC and adder eligibility should be verified with tax counsel before Safe Harbor commitment.
